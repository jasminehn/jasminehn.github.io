About Me

Hi, my name is Jasmine! I am a fourth year student at John Carroll University studying computer science. My interest in software and web development began in 2013, when I began creating custom Tumblr themes using HTML and CSS. Fast-forward to today, I have a variety of experience in software development, systems administration, and technical writing. I have had the amazing opportunity to intern with amazing companies like NASA, Code3, and Breakthrough Schools. I enjoy learning and discovering ways to create seamless user experiences using clean, efficient, and scalable code.

My interests lie in software engineering, UI/UX design, cybersecurity, machine learning, and innovation of next generation platforms. Work is an ongoing education for me, and I'm always searching for opportunities to collaborate with people who are as eager to share their expertise as I am to learn. Overall, my main goal is to use my skills to create something meaningful.
