About Me

Hi, I'm Jasmine! In 2022 I graduated from John Carroll University with a Bachelor's in Computer Science, and after that I had the amazing opportunity to work with companies like NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory. My interest in software and web development began in elementary school, when I began creating custom Tumblr themes using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Fast-forward to today, I have a variety of experience in full-stack development, web design, and unit testing. I enjoy learning and discovering ways to create seamless user experiences using clean, efficient, and scalable code.

My main interests are in front-end development, game design, spatial computing, and innovation of next generation platforms. Work is an ongoing education for me, and I'm always searching for opportunities to collaborate with people who are as eager to share their expertise as I am to learn. Overall, my main goal is to use my skills to create something meaningful.
